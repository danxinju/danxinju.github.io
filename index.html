App is dead

Who am I?

I am from the future and I would like to tell you: app is dead and web is in full bloom.

Reason

  • App is closed, like a lonely island. You can't link other apps easily and the content can not be indexed by the search engine.
  • App is expensive, we need to find it in the app market and download a big install file, data traffic is precious. Especially the Android, so many app markets makes me crazy.
  • App is boring, we already have enough notifications in my life, battery is precious. Life is short, we don't need to spend time on the settings and cache cleaning.
  • App is terrible, like a bloodsucker will upload the video, photo, document and file privately to the own server, evesdropping is unethical!
  • App is complex, you need to employ the IOS and Android engineers and do double work to build the app. Undoubtedly the cost is very expensive, especially for a small company.

Better Web

  • Web standards are making the compatibility work less and less, code once, run anywhere.
  • HTML(5+) is making the functionality stronger and stronger.
  • Hardware technology is making the performance better and better.
  • Sandbox is safe, code can access nothing from your device without the authorization.
  • Open information is making the universal database(like Google Search) is larger and larger.
I know most of app is the native right now, but it is not the future. No hurry, you only remember: most important factor is the Open and Standard.
